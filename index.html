Liuqian Wang 王柳谦

Tenure-Track Associate Professor, School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Zhengzhou University

I am currently a tenure-track associate professor with the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Zhengzhou University. I joined Zhengzhou University in 2026. From 2019 to 2025, I pursued a combined M.S.-Ph.D. program at Beijing University of Technology and received the Ph.D. degree in Engineering, conducting research at the Beijing Key Laboratory of Computational Intelligence and Intelligent System.

My research interests include remote sensing image perception and understanding, cross-view geo-localization, embodied intelligence, visual localization, and visual generation.

I am actively recruiting graduate students in computer vision and multimodal learning. Undergraduate students are also welcome to join our group as research interns or research assistants. Our group is committed to mentoring motivated students, including those without prior research experience, and has helped several students complete their first top-tier conference paper.
